AboutASPIRE

The 6th edition of ASPIRE, the Annual Social Entrepreneurship Conclave organized by ENACTUS NSUT is aimed at being a unique platform for young entrepreneurs to meet each other and interact with brilliant corporate and business minds. It is a one-of-a-kind entrepreneurship convention, which invites budding innovators in the social sector who want to make a positive change in the society through entrepreneurial action.
With a footfall of 1000+ during a period of 2 days of the convention, this year, with your support we are determined to make it even bigger and better. Last year, ASPIRE got participation from over 40 colleges in Delhi (IITD, DTU, St. Stephens, LSR, SSCBS, SRCC to name a few) and more colleges are expected to participate this year.

SAMADHAN

In the world full of an infinity of problems, let's start solving one at a time. Samadhan, the social case study competition of Aspire’19 takes you at the centre of a real life crisis. The competition encourages participants to brainstorm over a socially relevant problem. Participants are required to come up with innovative ideas and ingenuity in approach to decipher the problem. Various aspects such as compatibility , effectiveness and impact of the solution must be pondered upon which resonates with arriving at real life practical solutions, hence promoting the spirit of social entrepreneurship.

Rules-

  1. Team size 1-3 members
  2. Cross college teams are allowed.


  • Deadline for registration : 22nd January
  • Date of event- 9th February
  • Prelim case release- 16th January
  • Prelim submission- 23rd January
  • Final round case release- 28th January
